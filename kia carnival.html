The 2022 Kia Carnival is capable of having 11 seats in it however, there is one big caveat. The variant of the Carnival with 11 seats is actually called the Grand Carnival. Unfortunately, it is not yet available in the United States. Eleven seats come standard on the Kia Carnival in Thailand currently, and according to Kia’s website, there are dealers in Dominica offering the Grand Carnival. None are listed in the US, Canada, Mexico, or Europe. This seems like the perfect vehicle for anyone with a large family, so when can you expect it?
